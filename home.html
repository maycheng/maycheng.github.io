About Me

I'm a UX Designer based out of Chicago and I strive to balance user needs with business goals.

My curiosity drives me to seek a deeper understanding of things–which is why I love to channel key research insights into critical input toward meaningful solutions. What motivates me to problem solve is the empowerment that comes from serving as a voice for users and thinking critically about an issue.

My mind innately moves from exploring to producing because I thrive in the design-thinking process. Here’s my approach: listen to learn, think before writing, sketch and tell, then build and iterate. With my student advising background and UX training at DESIGNATION, I’m able to empathize with and design for users at every touchpoint.

When I’m not asking a ton of questions, I can be found indulging in retail therapy, training with my dance team or traveling off the beaten path in a new country.
