MileagePlus Premier status qualification update

Challenge

As one of the most rewarding frequent flyer programs in the world, the update to how members would qualify for status was a highly visible initiative impacting a loyalist user base for a global airline. I was tasked with leading the experience strategy and product design of the updated member account experience.

Approach

Process

  • Stakeholder interviews to gather requirements and refine scope
  • Audit existing state and conduct landscape analysis of comparative loyalty accounts

Design

  • Cross-collaborative ideation sessions to concept solutions
  • Introduce new contextual benefits feature within program update
  • Stakeholder reviews to drive business alignment and tech feasibility

Deliver

  • Member account onboarding for program updates
  • Accessible and responsive design for web and mobile app
