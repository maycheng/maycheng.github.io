Role

Deliverables

Background

United MileagePlusÂ® rewards its highest status level members with an annual allocation of complimentary upgrades for domestic or international flights. In 2019, the frequent flyer program introduced a new upgrade currency, transitioning from a certificate system based on region, to a more flexible points currency, customizable down to the class of service and flight segment.

Reference: MileagePlus PlusPoints Upgrades

Challenge

As one of the most generous upgrade benefits offered among U.S, global airlines, the introduction of PlusPoints was an enterprise-wise initiative with direct impact to the highest revenue-driving customer base for United. The project scaled across almost every cross-functional team within the company. In a hybrid design team, we were tasked with reimagining the upgrade benefit across flight booking, trip planning, and account management on united.com.

Approach

Kickoff

  • Kickoff to align on scope and define a strategy
  • SME workshops to gather requirements and influence program experience

Design process

  • 2-week sprints to evolve from initial concepts into detailed design
  • Create new global components leveraging a WIP design system
  • Usability and validation testing

Handoff

  • Program education for members
  • Accessible and responsive design on new service platform

Existing state

A traveler can request an upgrade for their upcoming flight and for their upgrade benefit, the currency is a Regional Upgrade Certificate (RPU)/Global Upgrade Certificate (GPU).
