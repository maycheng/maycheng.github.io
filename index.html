About Me

I’m a Chicago-based UX designer originally from Brooklyn, NY. I have a distinct love for creatively solving problems–which is why I’ve spent years in related fields advocating for the end user.

With empathy, optimism, and collaboration, I acted as a catalyst for change within organizations to design better ways of preparing learners for college and to study abroad. Advising learners trained me to drive user needs while balancing business goals, so UX training at DESIGNATION, a full-stack digital design bootcamp, was a natural transition in my career. Now, I’m able to apply my process-driven approach to designing for users at each touchpoint of digital products too.

When I’m not asking a ton of questions, I can be found training with my Latin dance team, working on a DIY project, or traveling off the beaten path in a new country.
