About me

I’m a UX Product Designer with a background in public service and education. I have a distinct passion for making my corner of the world a better place through user-centered design.

With my work in direct service, I acted as a catalyst for change within organizations to reshape learning programs for greater impact. Now, I channel this thoughtful approach toward understanding problems and designing solutions for human touch points in the digital experience. I’m a resourceful creative and a practical optimist, motivated by the drive to make a lasting impact with my work.

When I’m not asking a ton of questions, I can be found working on a DIY project, traveling to a new country, or taking another step towards minimalist living.
